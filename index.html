About Us

At Seasons and Harvest Agro-Allied Limited, we are more than just an agricultural business—we are a bridge between the richness of the land and the needs of the world.
Rooted in Nigeria’s fertile soil and driven by a commitment to excellence, we specialize in the processing, sale, and export of high-quality food crops, as well as the supply of essential raw materials like coal to industries across the globe.
Founded with a vision to add value to Nigeria’s vast agricultural potential, Seasons and Harvest Agro-Allied Limited was built on the principles of sustainability, quality, and integrity.
We are a proudly Nigerian-owned company working at the intersection of tradition and innovation to deliver premium agricultural products and raw materials to both local and international markets.
Our name—Seasons and Harvest—reflects our deep respect for nature’s cycles and our dedication to harvesting the best of what each season offers.
From farm to final product, we ensure that every item we handle meets the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and traceability.

OUR PRODUCTS

Ogbono Seeds

A Rich, Nutritious Staple in West African Cuisine Ogbono, also known as Bush Mango Seeds, is a treasured ingredient native to Nigeria and celebrated across West Africa. These nutrient-rich seeds are packed with protein, essential fats, and vitamins, making them not only a flavorful addition to meals but also a powerful source of nourishment. Our premium Ogbono seeds are carefully sourced and expertly processed to preserve their natural aroma, taste, and thickening properties. When used in traditional soups , Ogbono releases a deliciously nutty flavor and creates a smooth, hearty consistency that brings depth to every dish. Perfect for preparing beloved West African recipes like Ogbono Soup, these seeds offer both culinary delight and nutritional value in every spoonful.

Melon Seeds

Melon seeds, commonly known as Egusi in West Africa, are a prized agricultural product with deep cultural and culinary roots. Sourced from Nigeria’s fertile lands, these seeds are not only a cornerstone of traditional cooking but also a powerhouse of nutrition. Rich in plant-based protein, healthy fats, vitamin B-complex, and essential minerals like magnesium and zinc, melon seeds support everything from heart health and immune function to muscle repair and energy metabolism. They are a natural choice for health-conscious consumers and food lovers alike. We apply the highest standards in food processing to ensure that every batch of Melon-Seeds retains its natural aroma, nutty flavor, and nutritional value. Our premium-grade melon seeds are cleaned, sorted, and packaged with precision—making them ideal for both local and international markets.

Cashew Nuts

Cashew nuts are one of the world’s most sought-after tree nuts—valued for their rich, buttery flavor, smooth texture, and powerful nutritional profile. Sourced from the finest cashew-producing regions, our cashew nuts are processed with care to meet the highest standards of purity, safety, and quality. Cashew nuts are nutrient-densed superfood. Packed with heart-healthy fats, plant-based protein, and essential minerals like magnesium, copper, and zinc, they support brain function, bone health, and immune strength. Naturally cholesterol-free and low in sugar, cashews are an ideal choice for modern, health-conscious consumers. Our cashew nuts are carefuzlly cleaned, roasted, and graded to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. Our processing methods preserve the natural oils and delicate crunch of the nut, resulting in a premium product that meets the expectations of both domestic and international buyers.

Sesame Seeds

Sesame seeds are known for their distinct nutty flavor, versatility in cooking, and exceptional nutritional value. Sourced from trusted farms and carefully processed to preserve their purity and potency, our sesame seeds meet the highest standards for quality, cleanliness, and consistency. These tiny seeds are packed with essential nutrients—including calcium, iron, magnesium, zinc, and healthy fats—making them a powerful ingredient for promoting heart health, bone strength, and immune support. Our premium sesame seeds are meticulously sorted, cleaned, and packaged using advanced processing techniques to ensure uniform size, color, and freshness. From local markets to international exports, our sesame seeds are trusted by partners who value reliability, traceability, and excellence in every shipment.

Dried vegetable leaves consisting of Utazi,ukazi and bitterleaf

We proudly offer expertly processed dried Utazi, Ukazi, and Bitterleaf—three traditional African leaves celebrated for their rich nutritional and medicinal qualities. Grown sustainably and dried naturally, our leaves preserve authentic flavors and potent health benefits, making them favorites among chefs and consumers worldwide.
Utazi (Gongronema latifolium): Adds a bittersweet flavor to dishes and supports digestion, blood sugar regulation, reduces inflammation, and aids postpartum recovery.
Ukazi (Gnetum africanum): A fiber-rich leaf that improves digestion, supports heart health, provides protein and iron, and promotes eye health.
Bitterleaf (Vernonia amygdalina): Known for its strong flavor and healing properties, it helps manage blood sugar, fights infections, detoxifies liver and kidneys, and boosts immunity.
All our leaves are carefully harvested, thoroughly washed, and dried using gentle, natural methods to ensure they retain their nutritional integrity. Available in whole or shredded forms, our dried leaves meet stringent quality standards for both local consumption and international export.

COAL

Our premium coal is sustainably sourced and expertly processed to deliver high heat output, low smoke, and long-lasting burn time—making it a top choice for households, restaurants, barbecues, and traditional cooking methods across Africa and beyond. Crafted from natural hardwood, our coal undergoes a controlled carbonization process that preserves its purity and energy efficiency, resulting in clean-burning, low-ash fuel. It’s ideal for cooking, grilling, heating, and even for use in organic farming and industrial applications. Whether for local use or international distribution, our processed coal is a reliable, eco-friendly fuel solution that combines efficiency, affordability, and quality—perfect for homes, restaurants, and industrial buyers alike.
